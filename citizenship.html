Turkish official gazette (10.09.2018) states that any foreigner purchasing or owning real estate of a required value in Turkey is now eligible for Turkish Citizenship.

Starting from 12.06.2022, the required value of the property will be 400,000 USD.

CITIZENSHIP TYPES IN TURKEY

MARRIAGE

Foreigners can obtain citizenship after 3 years of marriage to a Turkish national. Certain conditions apply eg posing no threat to national security or public order.

PROPERTY AND RESIDENCE PERMIT HOLDERS

Any property owner in Turkey who has lived in the country for a straight, uninterrupted period of 5 years (article 11 of Act 3901) can apply.

THE TURQUOISE SYSTEM (2017)

This allows people who have skills or attributes in technology, economics, sports, culture and arts to apply for an alternative type of citizenship.

CITIZENSHIP BY INVESTMENT

People who have bought a property worth at least 400,000 USD or deposited 500,000 USD in Turkish banks are eligible to apply.
